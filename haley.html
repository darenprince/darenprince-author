Plain English for Haley

The symptom pattern may reflect a dangerous internal cause, not only skin-level inflammation. Because leg swelling and breathing symptoms are occurring together, ER evaluation is the safe path now.

Next actions

  1. Go to ER with clot and chest-testing capability.
  2. State: progressive swelling + intermittent shortness of breath.
  3. Provide prior labs and symptom timeline.
  4. Disclose stimulant/alcohol/smoking exposure.
  5. Ask to rule out clot, heart strain, and kidney injury first.

Self care while getting help

Harm reduction only. Not a substitute for emergency care.
  • Stop amphetamines immediately.
  • Avoid alcohol.
  • Avoid NSAIDs unless clinician-approved.
  • Avoid aggressive fluid loading if breathing worsens.
  • Avoid compression wraps until clot is excluded.
