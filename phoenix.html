Last Resort Safety Plan

  • If no shelter is available, prioritize visibility and safety.
  • Hospitals are safer than isolated streets.
  • Stay near lighting, cameras, bathrooms, and other people.
  • Avoid abandoned buildings, isolated woods, and hidden areas.
  • Keep your phone charged.
  • Conserve battery.
  • Keep belongings close.
  • Avoid conflict.
  • Get water first, calories second, rest third.
  • One safe night matters.

The goal tonight is not to solve your entire life. The goal is to stay alive, stay safe, and wake up with options.
