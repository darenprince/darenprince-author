What it is

Phoenix helps founders, sales teams, and operators reclaim lost opportunities by systematizing follow-up.

The app prioritizes who to contact, when to contact them, and how to message with confidence.

Its core value is converting forgotten leads into real outcomes without adding operational chaos.

Who it’s for

  • Founders managing lean sales pipelines
  • Small growth teams that need better follow-through
  • Advisors and operators tracking high-value relationships

Core capabilities

  • Lead revival scoring
  • Automated follow-up cadences
  • Template and tone recommendations
  • Task board and SLA reminders
  • Pipeline analytics dashboards
  • CRM export compatibility

What it is NOT

  • Not a full CRM replacement
  • Not spam automation
  • Not a guaranteed close engine
