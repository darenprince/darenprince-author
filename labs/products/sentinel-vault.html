What it is

Sentinel Vault gives teams a private operational vault for storing and sharing sensitive files with accountability built in from day one.

Every action is timestamped, permissioned, and exportable so compliance and legal review can happen without chaos.

The product is designed for security teams, founders, and advisors who need trusted evidence workflows without enterprise bloat.

Who it’s for

  • Security leads and operations teams handling sensitive incidents
  • Founders needing executive-grade document control
  • Legal, insurance, and compliance coordinators

Core capabilities

  • Tamper-evident document storage
  • Role-based access controls
  • Immutable event logging
  • Secure expiring-share links
  • Evidence export bundles
  • Cross-device vault access

What it is NOT

  • Not a consumer cloud drive replacement
  • Not a public collaboration workspace
  • Not a substitute for legal counsel
