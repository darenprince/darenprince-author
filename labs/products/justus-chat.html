What it is

JustUs Chat is a private communication app built around trust, tone, and accountability rather than endless noisy feeds.

It combines encrypted chat with guided prompts to help couples and close circles communicate better during both calm and conflict.

The product includes progress-oriented insights so users can track communication patterns over time.

Who it’s for

  • Couples seeking healthier communication habits
  • Therapists and coaches offering relationship support tools
  • Private communities that value trust-first messaging

Core capabilities

  • Secure direct and group messaging
  • Guided conversation prompts
  • Mood and tone check-ins
  • Shared journal moments
  • Relationship progress dashboards
  • Cross-platform notification controls

What it is NOT

  • Not a social media platform
  • Not anonymous chat
  • Not a replacement for licensed therapy
