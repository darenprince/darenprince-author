# Crown SOS Architecture

## Purpose

This document defines the architectural structure, crisis coordination infrastructure, survivability focused communication systems, and continuity response framework direction for Crown SOS.

The objective is to establish a scalable emergency coordination ecosystem capable of supporting live communication workflows, protected evidence continuity, proof of life systems, operational routing, and survivability focused response infrastructure.

## Core System Philosophy

Crown SOS is designed around:

- operational clarity
- survivability focused workflows
- continuity under stress
- rapid coordination systems
- protected communication infrastructure
- scalable browser based accessibility

Traditional emergency communication systems are often fragmented and reactive.

Crown SOS prioritizes unified continuity focused operational infrastructure.
