Project catalogue

Crown Labs active concepts

Each project tile breaks down the customer promise, active build track, and what has to be proven before the next release. Status language is refreshed whenever prototypes, pilots, or handbooks meaningfully evolve.

Pic Detective

Evidence-verification kit for investigators who need a fast read on whether a photo or still was edited, staged, or AI-generated.

  • Focus: Correlating EXIF timelines, hash lookups, and diffusion-artifact scans into an analyst-ready briefing.
  • Status: Browser prototype is running through internal case studies with subject-matter advisors.
  • Next step: Publish a field workbook and invite newsroom partners to stress test the workflow.

Ai Cherry Pie

Creative brief co-pilot that assembles audience research, brand voice, and launch logistics into ready-to-edit campaign recipes.

  • Focus: Building prompt "slices" for headlines, nurture sequences, and asset shot lists that stay on voice.
  • Status: Conversation maps and tone guides reviewed with marketing collaborators.
  • Next step: Release an interactive sample pantry with proven prompt stacks.

Crown SOS

Crisis coordination platform for survivor-led networks to confirm safety, activate mutual aid, and surface trusted resources.

  • Focus: Drafting escalation trees, geofenced check-ins, and warm handoff scripts for responders.
  • Status: Mobile and web wireframes undergoing tabletop exercises with partner organizations.
  • Next step: Validate notification cadences during a supervised emergency drill.

JustUs.chat

Moderated group messaging designed for restorative conversations where facilitators need trauma-aware controls.

  • Focus: Session agendas, reflection prompts, and receipt tracking that reinforce community agreements.
  • Status: Interview insights translated into a requirements doc and facilitation playbook.
  • Next step: Build a clickable prototype covering onboarding, moderation, and consent renewals.

CrownCode.ai

Automation operations hub that turns repeatable build standards into reusable prompt packs and test harnesses.

  • Focus: Cataloging delivery checklists, RAG templates, and evaluation scorecards for internal teams.
  • Status: Active in-house system guiding how experiments ship across Crown Labs projects.
  • Next step: Draft contributor guidelines and open a limited-access partner workspace.

Operation Phoenix

Structured recovery program for survivors and advocates who need reliable rituals, planning cadences, and peer accountability.

  • Focus: Weekly reset ceremonies, micro-journaling prompts, and progress audits that rebuild stability.
  • Status: Version 1 handbook published with ongoing cohort feedback collection.
  • Next step: Compile case studies and integrate trauma specialist reviews for v2.
Working style

How Crown Labs tests ideas

Crown Labs focuses on small, evidence-based iterations. Research notes, prototypes, and partner pilots are documented before any public launch commitments are made.

Research sessions

Interviews and case studies capture how survivors, advocates, and builders intend to use each tool.

Prototype reviews

Low- and mid-fidelity builds are reviewed with trusted advisors to ensure the scope matches real needs.

Measured releases

Pilots are documented with clear support plans before scaling or inviting additional collaborators.
