# CrownCast Licensing

## Purpose

This document defines the licensing structures, deployment models, engagement integration opportunities, and intellectual property protection strategy for CrownCast.

## Licensing Philosophy

CrownCast is designed as both:

- a standalone alignment platform
- a modular emotionally aware engagement infrastructure layer

This structure supports:

- direct deployment
- awareness ecosystems
- continuity focused engagement environments
- scalable reflection systems

## Consumer Licensing

Consumer licensing may include:

- individual subscriptions
- premium awareness features
- reflection systems
- emotionally aware engagement workflows

## Enterprise Licensing

Enterprise licensing structures may include:

- awareness systems
- continuity focused engagement infrastructure
- behavioral reflection environments
- scalable interaction systems

Enterprise agreements may include:

- organization wide deployment
- managed engagement infrastructure
- awareness coordination systems
- deployment support environments

## Intellectual Property Protection

Licensing structures should preserve:

- platform ownership
- engagement infrastructure ownership
- interaction workflow ownership
- ecosystem integration rights

## Long Term Licensing Strategy

The long term objective is to establish CrownCast as:

- a trusted alignment platform
- a scalable reflection ecosystem
- a reusable awareness infrastructure layer
- a continuity focused engagement environment

while maintaining strong clarity and scalable infrastructure design.
