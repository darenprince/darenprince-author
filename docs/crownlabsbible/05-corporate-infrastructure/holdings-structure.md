# Holdings Structure

## Purpose

This document defines the organizational structure, intellectual property relationships, ecosystem hierarchy, operational ownership framework, and strategic portfolio architecture for Crown Labs and Daren Prince Holdings.

The objective is to establish a scalable institutional structure capable of supporting ecosystem interoperability, operational governance, licensing management, valuation organization, and long term infrastructure expansion.

## Parent Structure

Daren Prince Holdings functions as the overarching ownership and strategic governance entity supporting:

- Crown Labs
- intellectual property ownership
- portfolio governance
- licensing infrastructure
- operational ecosystem management
- strategic expansion systems

The holdings structure is intentionally designed around coordinated infrastructure ecosystems rather than isolated software entities.

## Crown Labs Role

Crown Labs functions as the primary operational technology and infrastructure ecosystem inside the broader holdings structure.
