# Sentinel Vault Monetization

## Purpose

This document defines the monetization structure, enterprise opportunities, ecosystem revenue influence, subscription potential, and long term commercial strategy for Sentinel Vault.

The objective is to position Sentinel Vault as a scalable survivability and continuity infrastructure ecosystem with diversified monetization pathways and strong operational positioning.

## Core Monetization Philosophy

Sentinel Vault is intentionally designed around long term continuity infrastructure rather than short term consumer utility behavior.

The platform architecture supports:

- recurring subscriptions
- secure infrastructure services
- enterprise deployment opportunities
- survivability focused feature expansion
- operational continuity systems
- ecosystem reinforcement

This creates long term strategic value beyond simple cloud storage monetization.

## Subscription Infrastructure

The primary monetization model is subscription based.

Potential subscription categories include:

### Essential

Protected storage infrastructure and continuity focused account systems.

### Advanced

Expanded evidence preservation systems, survivability workflows, emergency coordination infrastructure, and protected media systems.

### Premium

Advanced continuity systems, trusted contact workflows, disclosure infrastructure, secure communication systems, and expanded survivability features.

### Enterprise

Organization level continuity systems, operational deployment infrastructure, secure routing systems, and managed support environments.
