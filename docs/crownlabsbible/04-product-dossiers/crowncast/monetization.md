# CrownCast Monetization

This document defines the monetization structure, subscription pathways, ecosystem revenue influence, emotionally aware engagement positioning, and long term commercial strategy for CrownCast.

The objective is to position CrownCast as a scalable emotionally aware reflection and alignment ecosystem with diversified monetization pathways and strong behavioral engagement infrastructure positioning.

CrownCast is intentionally designed around awareness, reflection, alignment, and continuity focused engagement rather than passive motivational consumption.

- recurring subscriptions
