This document defines the strategic valuation positioning, monetization influence, licensing potential, and long term commercial outlook for AI Cherry Pie.

The objective is to maintain realistic but strategically informed valuation logic.

Category: Writing reconstruction and language refinement platform

Type: Standalone executable software product and reusable framework

Positioning: Humanization engine and AI fingerprint reduction platform
